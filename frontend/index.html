    How to Play

    Quick start guide

    • Join a room or create one to start playing.
    • Use chat to discuss and vote on who to eliminate.
    • When the game starts, your role will be revealed to you.

    Role guide

    • Villager: No special night action. Use discussion and voting to find Wolves.
    • Wolf: At night, choose a non-Wolf player to eliminate.
    • Seer: At night, inspect one player to learn their exact role.
    • Healer: At night, choose one player to protect from the Wolf attack.
    • Little Girl: At night, peek to see the Wolves, but there is a high chance they notice you.
    • Artemis: At night, you may shoot one player or skip. Hit a Wolf and they die; hit an innocent and you die instead.
